Music

If there is one thing I would place above all else, it is music. When I was younger, I didn't really listen to music. It wasn't until middle school that I really started to get into music. In my freshman year of high school, I got my first guitar, and ever since then, I've been obsessed. When senior year rolled around, I joined a band as a bass guitarist. While we never made our own songs, it was a great way to express how we felt.

When it comes to listening to music, I enjoy just about every genre. I like to say that I'm pretty open to anything musically. My favorite artists include:

  • Frank Ocean
  • Kanye West
  • Paramore
  • The Weeknd
  • Post Malone
  • A$AP Rocky
  • Dua Lipa
